## Purpose
Define capability lifecycle and long-term platform evolution governance for ECOM OS.

## External practice alignment
This strategy follows:
- lifecycle governance;
- platform stewardship;
- controlled evolution;
- capability deprecation;
- backwards-compatible transitions.

## Capability lifecycle
Capabilities may move through:
- IDEA;
- DOCUMENTED;
- DRY_RUN;
- READ_ONLY;
- LIMITED_LIVE;
- STABLE;
- DEPRECATED;
- RETIRED.

## Evolution rules
Platform evolution should:
- preserve governance principles;
- preserve audit continuity;
- preserve replay compatibility;
- preserve inventory truth integrity;
- preserve security boundaries.

## Deprecation governance
Deprecated capability should:
- remain traceable;
- preserve historical interpretation;
- define migration path;
- define retirement date where appropriate.

## STOP conditions
STOP if:
- capability lifecycle unclear;
- deprecation breaks replay/history;
- platform evolution bypasses governance;
- migration path missing.
