## Purpose
Define long-term scalability and survivability governance for ECOM OS.

## External practice alignment
This strategy follows:
- platform survivability;
- scalability engineering;
- operational durability;
- maintainability governance;
- resilience-aware growth.

## Core principle
The platform should remain operable, governable, and recoverable as complexity grows.

## Survivability dimensions
The platform should preserve:
- governance integrity;
- inventory truth integrity;
- replay continuity;
- audit continuity;
- operational predictability;
- upgrade survivability;
- recovery effectiveness.

## Scalability guidance
Growth should not:
- destroy observability;
- bypass governance;
- split inventory truth;
- disable replay/recovery;
- create uncontrolled coupling.

## Long-term maintenance
The platform should support:
- controlled upgrades;
- operational onboarding;
- recoverable failures;
- replayable history;
- migration-aware evolution.

## STOP conditions
STOP if:
- scaling destroys governance;
- complexity removes recoverability;
- platform evolution becomes unpredictable;
- operational survivability declines.
