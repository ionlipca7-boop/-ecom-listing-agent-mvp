
## 5. Item specifics lesson

The experiment showed that eBay's internal price/value comparison appears highly sensitive to the full combination of category, item specifics, title, photos, and description.

Fields with visible eBay search-count signals should be treated as high-priority ranking/value signals. In this listing, fields observed as important included:

```text
Produktart ~25,366 Suchen

Anzeigetyp ~6,329 Suchen
```

## 7. Gallery / image-generation lesson

Real product photos must be the truth layer. Supplier images can only be used for polished visual direction, not for package contents or accessories.

Final successful gallery logic:

1. Hero image: tester + manual + metal box, badge `Ohne Zubehör-Kit`, no fake accessory kit.
2. Product overview: connector callouts for USB-A, USB-C, Micro-USB, DC5.5, Type-C input/output, USB output, Power Supply/NTC.
3. Detail focus: M/+ button, OK/- button, power indicator, color display, control side.
4. Technical specs: only rear-label-based specs.
5. How-to-use: source -> tester -> device/load.
6. Lifestyle: desk/workstation use, monitor charging.
7. Scenario image: USB-Netzteil, Powerbank, Smartphone/USB-Gerät, DC5.5-Quelle — careful, no universal compatibility claim.
8. Storage image: tester in metal box.
9. Lieferumfang: only tester, metal box, manual.
10. Final benefits summary: Farbdisplay, USB/DC-Anschlüsse, Metallbox inklusive, kompaktes Messgerät.

Image prompt rule:

- Preserve physical geometry and port layout.
- Use German text only on images.
- Maintain unified white/light premium background with black/gold accents.
- Do not show unconfirmed accessory bundles.
- Do not claim `full kit`.
- Do not create wrong connector directions.
- For technical diagrams, use real device labels and conservative German callouts.

## 8. Title experiment lesson

Working value-signal formula:

```text
[Brand] + [Model] + [USB DC Messgerät] + [Bluetooth] + [Power Meter] + [Spannung Strom] + [PD QC]
```

Best observed title:

```text
ATORCH UD24 USB DC Messgerät Bluetooth Power Meter Spannung Strom PD QC
```

Alternative test variants for future:

```text
ATORCH UD24 USB DC Bluetooth Power Meter Messgerät Spannung Strom PD QC
ATORCH UD24 USB DC Digital Tester Bluetooth Power Meter PD QC Messgerät
ATORCH UD24 USB DC Tester Bluetooth Voltmeter Amperemeter Power Meter
```

Weak/generic direction that lowered value:

```text
USB Tester für Ladegerät Kabel...
USB Messgerät für Ladegerät Kabel...
Generic Voltmeter/Amperemeter-only title without UD24/Bluetooth/Power Meter/PD QC
```

Interpretation:

- Generic buyer-friendly titles may increase broad relevance but can reduce perceived value by moving the product into cheap accessory clusters.
- For technical products, title must combine simple search terms with premium technical identity.
- Brand/model can help if it identifies a known technical product class.
- Bluetooth and Power Meter were important value signals for this product.
